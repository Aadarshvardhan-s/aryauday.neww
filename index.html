Who We Are

Rooted in Dharma,
Rising for Bharat

ARYAUDAY is a registered NGO committed to the holistic development of Indian society through ethical, social, and cultural transformation. We believe every citizen of Bharat deserves dignity, opportunity, and protection.

📚

Free Child Education

Ensuring quality education for underprivileged children across Madhya Pradesh.

🌱

Youth Development

Empowering young Bharat through sports, skills, and leadership programs.

👵

Elderly Care

🛡️

Women's Safety & Gau Raksha

🏛️

🤝

Humanitarian Service

Our Work

Core Wings of ARYAUDAY

Four powerful pillars that form the foundation of our transformative work across Bharat.

01
📖

Free Child Education

We run free schools, tuition centers, and literacy programs for children from marginalized communities, ensuring no child is left behind due to poverty.

02
🏆

Sports Promotion

Identifying and nurturing athletic talent in rural Bharat. We provide training, equipment, and competition opportunities for young athletes.

03
🧓

Elderly Support

Comprehensive care programs for senior citizens including health check-ups, emotional support, financial aid, and community activities.

04
🕌

Cultural & Temple Development

Restoration and development of temples and cultural heritage sites. Organizing festivals, workshops, and cultural events to preserve our traditions.

PRANV
Pratyangira Narsimha Vahini

Specialized Wing for Protection & Justice

PRANV is ARYAUDAY's elite specialized wing dedicated to women's safety, Gau Raksha, and legal justice — combining grassroots activism with institutional coordination.

👩‍⚖️

Women Safety

24/7 helpline, legal aid, and protection network for women in distress across Madhya Pradesh.

🐄

Gau Raksha

Active protection of sacred cows through rescue operations, shelters, and law enforcement coordination.

⚖️

Law Coordination

Bridging the gap between citizens and law enforcement for swift justice and resolution.

Leadership

The Vision Behind ARYAUDAY

🙏
Founder
Yashvardhan Rai Shrivastav

Yashvardhan Rai Shrivastav is a dedicated social entrepreneur and activist who founded ARYAUDAY with a vision to create holistic and lasting change in Bharatiya society. Born with a deep sense of dharma and service, he has dedicated his life to uplifting the marginalized, protecting the vulnerable, and preserving the rich cultural heritage of India.

Under his visionary leadership, ARYAUDAY has grown from a small initiative in Madhya Pradesh into a recognized force for social transformation, touching thousands of lives across education, women's safety, elderly care, Gau Raksha, and cultural revival.

"Seva is not charity — it is the highest expression of our dharmic identity. When we serve another, we serve the divine in them. ARYAUDAY is not just an NGO; it is a movement of awakened souls committed to building a stronger, prouder Bharat."
📍 Madhya Pradesh
🏛️ Social Entrepreneur
🎯 Dharmic Activist
